ABOUT US

Simplicity never goes out of style - this has been our philosophy at STAC and one of the many things that brought us together!
Our frustration with the lack of this simplicity in the fine cafe we owned made us think of ARCHIE's.

Why Archie's?

A good cafe name is the very first touchpoint of your business that your customers will experience.
You want the cafe name to be short & sweet, easy to pronounce, easy to spell, and most importantly easy to remember.
The last thing you want is customers misspelling your cafe name whilst searching for it on google or telling their friends about you.
